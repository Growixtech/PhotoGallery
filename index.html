About Galleria

Galleria is a modern photo gallery application that connects to the Unsplash API to bring you stunning, high-quality images from photographers around the world.

Features

  • Browse millions of photos from Unsplash
  • Search by categories and keywords
  • Multiple viewing modes
  • Voice search functionality
  • Bulk download capabilities
  • Personal album management
  • Search history tracking

Credits

All photos are provided by Unsplash, the internet's source of freely-usable images.
